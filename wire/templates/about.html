ABOUT EDROWIRE

EDROWIRE is an algorithmic news wire that monitors over 100 editorial sources in real time, clusters related coverage into unified story threads, and surfaces what matters based on breadth of coverage and source quality.

HOW IT WORKS

RSS feeds and search sweeps are polled continuously. Incoming headlines are clustered using TF-IDF similarity matching. Each cluster is scored by the number and quality of independent sources reporting the story, with time decay so breaking news rises fast and stale stories fall naturally.

Headlines are rewritten into Bloomberg-wire style by AI for consistency and clarity. Categories (Tech, Markets, Politics, World) are assigned automatically. Source quality tiers weight trusted outlets more heavily in the ranking algorithm.

EDITORIAL PHILOSOPHY

EDROWIRE has no editorial staff and takes no editorial positions. The algorithm prioritizes stories with broad, independent multi-source coverage from reputable outlets. Single-source stories require a minimum quality threshold to surface. The system is designed to reflect what the press is covering, not to decide what should be covered.
