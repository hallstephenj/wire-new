PRIVACY POLICY

Effective: February 23, 2026

Substantive Systems, LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates EDROWIRE. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

EDROWIRE is designed to minimize data collection. We collect:

  • Server logs: IP addresses, browser user-agent strings, and request timestamps are recorded in standard server logs for security and operational purposes.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics such as page views and popular categories.

We do not require account creation, collect personal information, or use tracking cookies.

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers through the main service.
  • We do not use third-party analytics trackers, advertising networks, or social media pixels.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. COOKIES

EDROWIRE does not set cookies. If cookies are introduced in the future for functional purposes (such as remembering display preferences), this policy will be updated accordingly.

4. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

EDROWIRE contains links to third-party news sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. DATA RETENTION

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days and then deleted. Aggregated usage statistics contain no personally identifiable information and may be retained indefinitely.

6. SECURITY

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

EDROWIRE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. CONTACT

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: legal@substantivesystems.com
